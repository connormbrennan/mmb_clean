Consensus Economics inflation expectations panel

Source and selection
- WRDS library/table: tr_ds_econ.wrds_ecoinfo joined to tr_ds_econ.ecodata
- Source filter: srccode == **20 (ZZ: CONSENSUS ECONOMICS INC.)
- Description terms: consumer prices, %yoy
- Main panel statistic: mean
- Main panel horizons: [1, 2]
- The script keeps announceddate as survey_date; perioddate is the target annual inflation year.
- Realized CPI series from the old notebook are excluded because they are not sourced to Consensus Economics and have CONPRC-style mnemonics.

Output files
- panel_csv: consensus_economics_inflation_expectations_panel.csv
- panel_parquet: consensus_economics_inflation_expectations_panel.parquet
- vintages_csv: consensus_economics_inflation_forecast_vintages.csv
- series_metadata_csv: consensus_economics_series_metadata.csv
- codebook_txt: consensus_economics_inflation_expectations_panel_codebook.txt
- availability_audit_txt: consensus_economics_wrds_availability_audit.txt

Main panel dimensions
- Rows: 21
- Columns: 13
- Countries: 1
- Survey date range: 2013-02-07 00:00:00 to 2015-12-03 00:00:00
- Target years: [2014, 2015, 2016]

Available Consensus Economics CPI forecast series found on WRDS
- ALCECPRM: ALBANIA, CONSUMER PRICES (%YOY, MEAN), ANNL, Percentage, statistic=mean
- NLCACPRS: NETHERLANDS, CONSUMER PRICES (%YOY, STDEV), ANNL, Percentage, statistic=stdev
